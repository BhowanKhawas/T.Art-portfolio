Tisa (Dilasha) Shrestha

Exploring the intersection of tradition and modern surrealism.

Artistic Statement

Tisa Shrestha is a visual artist specializing in a diverse range of mediums, from the disciplined world of classical charcoal study to the fluid, expressive nature of watercolor and ink.

Her work is characterized by a deep fascination with human emotion and cultural iconography. By blending the raw textures of traditional sketching with the bold, neon-lit aesthetics of contemporary illustration, she creates a visual bridge between the past and the present.

Expertise

  • Anatomical & Still Life Charcoal Study
  • Fluid Watercolor Seashcapes & Florals
  • Character Design & Modern Ink Illustration
  • Cultural Deity & Traditional Sketching

The Portfolio

This digital archive houses over 50 curated works, reflecting years of technical growth and creative discovery. Each piece is an exploration of form, light, and the narrative power of visual art.

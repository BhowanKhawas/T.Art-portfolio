## Artistic Statement
Tisa Shrestha is a visual artist specializing in a diverse range of mediums, from the disciplined world of classical charcoal study to the fluid, expressive nature of watercolor and ink. Her work is characterized by a deep fascination with human emotion and cultural iconography. By blending the raw textures of traditional sketching with the bold, neon-lit aesthetics of contemporary illustration, she creates a visual bridge between the past and the present.

## The Collection
This digital archive houses over 50 curated works, reflecting years of technical growth and creative discovery. Each piece is an exploration of form, light, and the narrative power of visual art.

## Expertise & Mediums
* **Charcoal Study**: Anatomical and still life explorations focusing on depth and shadow.
* **Watercolor & Ink**: Fluid seascapes, florals, and modern ink illustrations.
* **Modern Surrealism**: Character design and cultural deity sketching with a contemporary edge.

## Featured Works
* **Duality of the Divine**: A highly detailed monochromatic piece contrasting a serene, traditional profile with the fierce, intricate visage of a cultural deity.
* **The Cave's Horizon**: A striking painting depicting the sharp silhouette of a galleon emerging from a dark, textured cave into a vibrant, sunlit sea.
* **Silenced Emotions**: A raw, expressive charcoal portrait capturing internal dialogue through texture and line.
* **Citrus Vibrance**: A study in color and transparency through watercolor fruit segments.
